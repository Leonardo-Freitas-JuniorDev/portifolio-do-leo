Sobre mim

Leonardo started with Ethical Hacking and went deep into the area. After completing some courses, he entered the programming area. He started programming in Python and then moved to C++. Leonardo is currently a Front-end developer and is always looking to learn more.

Leonardo learned the complete Front-end on the Alura course platform. At the age of 17, Leonardo develops several personal websites to show the public and so, get his job as a Front-end developer in the heated market of technology.
